Alphabet Game

You are trying to find signs that have a word that starts with each letter of the alphabet. Only one person can use a word from each sign, so it's a race to find the words.


You must go in letter order - any sign will count. The first person to get through the whole alphabet wins.


So for instance you would start A- Altoona if you see a sign for Altoona, you then move onto B- BP, C- Car, etc. all the way to Z

Famous People

You can designate a category for this game like singers, actors, celebrities, athletes, etc.


What will happen is you will go around in order and each person will name a famous person- first and last name. The next person will have to name someone whose first name starts with the same letter as the last name of the person last named.


If the person on your left says Beyonce Knowles, then you will have to name someone who starts with a K- Kobe Bryant. The next person would have to name someone with a B and so on.


If you say a double letter like Janet Jackson (2 J's) then it reverses direction back to person before you.

Any time someone repeats a person, they are out. Keep playing until you have a winner.

Never Have I Ever

This game is best played with another car. One car will come up with something they think some of the people in the other car have done like skydiving for instance.


Car 2 will let them know how many of their passengers have done the thing (skydiving).


Car 1 will then have to guess who has done the thing (skydiving).


Then it's the other car's turn. Play until...you feel like stopping.

Caravan of 3...get creative.

Twenty Questions

One person states they are a person, place or thing. (Or declares animal, vegetable or mineral.)

Everybody else gets to interrogate the player with 20 questions, which must be answered "Yes" or "No."

License Plate Lingo

Make up a phrase using the letters on another car's plate.

Spot a license plate and call out the letters on it. Then everyone tries to come up with a different phrase using the letters in the order they appear on the license plate as the first letter of each word.

For instance, KEW could be "kittens eating watermelon" or "kiss every warthog."

When you can't think of any more, look for another license plate and start again!

Cow Game

In this game, you count cows along your route.


As you pass a herd of cows, players will should "Those are my cows!" The first one to say it starts counting the cows.


Once the car passes the cow, it can no longer be counted. Whoever has the most cows at the end of the trip wins.


Churches double your cows, graveyards halve an opponents cows of your choice, and horses count for 10 cows.

Song Game

Going around the car, each player will name a song starting with the letter of the alphabet.


For example, if the first player says "Ain't No Grave", the next player must say a song that starts with "B".


For extra fun, make a playlist of all the songs your car thinks of.

Fast-Food Frenzy

At the start of the trip, everyone picks a fast food restaurant (Not a gas station with food)


Every time you pass a restaurant, you get a point. Whoever has the most points by a set end wins.


Ending can be when you refuel, the end of the trip, when you stop to eat, etc...
